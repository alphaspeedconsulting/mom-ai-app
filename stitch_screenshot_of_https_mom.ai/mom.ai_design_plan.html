# mom.Ai App Design Plan ## Overview The mom.Ai app is designed to be a powerful assistant for busy moms, offering a suite of AI agents to handle everyday tasks and reduce mental load. ## Proposed Screens ### 1. Home / Discovery Screen * **Purpose:** The entry point for the app, focusing on discovering the right agent for the task at hand. * **Key Features:** * Search bar for agents/tasks. * Categorized list of agents (e.g., Household, Health, Education, Personal). * "Recommended for You" section based on recent activity. * Quick access to active/recently used agents. ### 2. Grocery & Meal Planning Assistant * **Purpose:** To streamline the recurring task of food management. * **Key Features:** * Interactive grocery list managed by an AI agent. * Meal planning suggestions based on dietary preferences. * Integration with popular grocery delivery services. * "What's in my pantry" tracker. ### 3. Family Calendar & Organizer * **Purpose:** To centralize and manage the complex schedules of a modern family. * **Key Features:** * Consolidated view of family appointments. * AI-driven conflict detection and rescheduling suggestions. * Task/to-do list integration. * Reminders for school events, extracurriculars, and appointments. ### 4. Agent Interaction Hub (Chat) * **Purpose:** The core interface for direct communication with AI agents. * **Key Features:** * Natural language chat interface. * Support for voice and text input. * Contextual actions (e.g., "Add to list", "Book this appointment"). * Clear feedback on agent status and actions.
