# Product Decision (Structured Options + Tradeoffs)

Act as a product-minded technical leader.

## GOALS
- Frame a product decision clearly
- Enumerate options and tradeoffs
- Recommend a path with explicit risks and mitigations

CONTEXT:
- Files/context: $ARGUMENTS
- Standards: `.cursorrules`, `CLAUDE.md`

## REQUIREMENTS
1. Clarify the decision question in one sentence.
2. Identify success metrics and constraints (time, budget, risk, implementation complexity).
3. Provide 3 to 5 realistic options.
4. For each option include:
   - Pros and cons
   - Risks
   - Stakeholder impact (who it helps or hurts)
5. Recommend one option and explain why.
6. Provide next steps describing what to validate and what to measure.

## OUTPUT
- Decision Framing
- Success Metrics and Constraints
- Options with Pros, Cons, and Risks
- Recommendation
- Next Steps
