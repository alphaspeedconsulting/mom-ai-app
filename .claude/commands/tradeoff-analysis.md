# Tradeoff Analysis (Decision Matrix)

Act as a senior engineer evaluating competing concerns.

## GOALS
- Make tradeoffs explicit and measurable
- Provide a clear recommendation with rationale
- Identify what would change the decision

CONTEXT:
- Files/context: $ARGUMENTS
- Standards: `.cursorrules`, `CLAUDE.md`

## REQUIREMENTS
1. State the decision and competing constraints.
2. List the primary dimensions being optimized (for example: speed, cost, risk, quality, maintainability, portability).
3. Provide at least 3 viable options.
4. Include:
   - A qualitative decision matrix
   - A short narrative explaining the major tradeoffs
5. Recommend one option and specify:
   - Key risks
   - Mitigations
   - What tests, logs, or metrics would validate the decision after rollout

## OUTPUT
- Decision and Constraints
- Options
- Tradeoff Matrix
- Recommendation
- Validation Plan
